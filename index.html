About Us

geethanjali yoga centre

Geethanjali Yoga Centre, nestled in the heart of Saram, Puducherry, is a dedicated wellness space where yoga is more than just exercise — it is a way of life. With a stellar rating of 4.9 out of 5, our centre is renowned for its personalised approach, ensuring every student receives the individual attention they deserve on their wellness journey. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, our doors are open every day of the week from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM to welcome you.
